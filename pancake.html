Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg with milk, vanilla, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and stir until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto the pan.
  6. Flip when bubbles appear on the surface. Cook until golden.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 250
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Protein: 6g
