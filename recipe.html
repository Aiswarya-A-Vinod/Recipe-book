Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix in spaghetti and stir well.
  5. Serve hot with grated cheese if desired.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbs: 60g
  • Fat: 8g
