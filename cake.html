Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and oil. Beat until the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 420
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Protein: 5g
